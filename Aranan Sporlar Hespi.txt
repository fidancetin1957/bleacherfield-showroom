## 🔮 2026 Forecast: Sports x Location (Spor ve Konum Tahmini)
*How the "Map/Location" niche will evolve within the Sports category.*

**The Trend:** **"Sacred Ground" & "Stadium Coordinates"**
Fans are moving away from just team logos to celebrating the *place* where the magic happens.

**Predicted High-Volume Search Terms (2026):**
1.  **"Stadium Coordinates Hoodie"**: Minimalist design with just the GPS coordinates of famous stadiums (e.g., Lambeau Field, Fenway Park).
2.  **"Tailgate Map T-shirt"**: Stylized maps of the parking lots/tailgate zones of major college football stadiums (Very niche, very passionate).
3.  **"Seat View Art"**: Graphics showing the view from specific iconic sections (e.g., "The Bleacher Creatures" at Yankee Stadium).
4.  **"City x Team Crossover"**: "Chicago is for Bears" style text-based location designs.
5.  **"Away Game Tracker Map"**: A "scratch-off" style design printed on apparel where people check off stadiums they've visited.

**Estimated Search Volume (Monthly):**
*   *Stadium Map Art:* 50,000 - 100,000
*   *Team City Vintage Shirt:* 20,000 - 40,000
*   *Stadium Coordinates:* 10,000 - 25,000
